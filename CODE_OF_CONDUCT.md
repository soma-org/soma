# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by its terms.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **`contact@soma.org`**.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is
obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
